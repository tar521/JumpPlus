They will be able to file a report and give you a document that you can use to travel back home. You will also need to contact your embassy or consulate in the Faroe Islands. They will be able to help you apply for a new passport.

Here are the steps on what to do if you lost your passport in the Faroe Islands:

Get a document from the police station that you can use to travel back home.
Contact your embassy or consulate in the Faroe Islands.
Apply for a new passport.
Pay the application fee.
Provide the necessary documentation.
Wait for your new passport to be processed.
Collect your new passport from your embassy or consulate.

Make a copy of your passport and keep it in a safe place.
Keep your passport with you at all times.
Don't leave your passport unattended.
